Web & Brand Design for Creative Entrepreneurs

A little bit about me.

I’m a designer and front-end web developer based out of Santa Cruz, California. I draw inspiration from nature, architecture, and the simple organic beauty that surrounds us. I mix my background of Web and Visual Design, Anthropology, Interior Design, and fine arts to build beautiful digital experiences.

Most days you can find me at my desk surrounded by mood boards, my Great Pyrenees puppy by my side, and a strong cup of coffee in hand. When I’m not working, I’m usually outside enjoying the sunshine with my handsome husband, planning our next getaway, or hiking in beautiful redwoods that surround our home in the Santa Cruz mountains.

Services

There are so many factors and elements involved in being an entrepreneur and business owner. Staying consistent across all platforms is key to maintaining
a professional, confident, and cohesive brand. Consistency is one of the most important factors to attracting your ideal client and building trust with them.
This is where our in-depth branding work comes in to create a seamless, cohesive, and intentional brand that your ideal client will be able to spot from a mile
away, no matter which platform they find you on.

Our website designs are created with you in mind, down to the very last detail. Every aspect has a purpose, from the formation of your logo, to the carefully
curated fonts we use for your brand, from the look and feel of your website, to the way your brand “speaks” to your ideal clients.
